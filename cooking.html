Cooking

I had been meaning to make my own cookbook for a good while. I like the idea of compiling all my favorite recipes with the tweaks I have made all into one place. For Christmas (2024) my Mum got be an empty cookbook ready to be filled in, so now I have no excuse not to.

For this page I am going to upload pictures from the book mostly because I don't want to write things twice but also to show off the book a little.

In 2024 I became lactose intolerant, this seemed to come out of nowhere, so all of these recipes will cater for that.
