Recommended Video Games

The unique element of video games is the interactivity. I tend to enjoy games that have more interactivity and have a greater gameplay focus than story focus. I also mostly play singleplayer games. There are a few things that will really take me out of a game like when control is taken away from the player especially when it is done often so I usually avoid games like this.
