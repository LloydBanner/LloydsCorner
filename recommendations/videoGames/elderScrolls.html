Elder Scrolls

Another one of my all time favorite series. A fantasy open world RPG series that always pulls me in. I love the setting, the lore and the gameplay. Some might call these games basic or simple, but I think the simplicity allows for the variety in quests and the great exploration we see in these games. I also think the worlds are incredibly detailed for their scale which really allows you to get in to the role of your character.

Other Elder Scrolls Stuff

I really want to play Daggerfall at some point as I have heard it is great when you get past some of the more dated elements. There is a port in to the unity engine which I have hear fixes a lot of the bugs and issues.
